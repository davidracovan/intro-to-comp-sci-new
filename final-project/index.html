Wearable devices are a group of devices that are designed to be worn throughout the day. Multiple types of wearables exist, though the most popular are smartwatches that sync to a smartphone. The two main companies in the business are Apple and Fitbit, each catering their watches towards different health benefits and groups of people.

Good smartwatches...

  • ...use GPS to track your location during workouts
  • ...track your heart rate while exercising
  • ...monitor your vitals during fitness
  • ...have sleep tracking
  • ...have EKG capabilities
