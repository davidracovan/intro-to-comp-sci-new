The Apple Watch is a wearable device that boasts a large amount of health features.

Features

  • Fitness tracking
  • Taking an EKG
  • Heart rate monitoring
  • Blood oxygen levels
  • Low fitness notifications

At $300-$400, the Apple Watch is not an inexpensive device. However, many see its vast number of benefits and ignore the large price tag.
