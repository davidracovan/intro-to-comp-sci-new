The Benefits of Gaming and Programming

Gaming and programming go hand in hand in offering benefits both to each other and to other activities.

Gaming

  • Improves reflexes for day-to-day activities
  • Improves hand-eye coordination
  • Allows for relaxation after school
  • Sharpens critical thinking

Programming

  • Makes you think from an analytical/logical perspective
  • Allows for better performance in games as you think from a developer's point of view
  • Improves knowledge of computers
  • Helps you solve real-world problems with software
  • Makes for a great experience in patience and problem-solving
